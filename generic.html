About Me

Want to know more about me? Here you go.

I'm currently in my senier year of highschool enjoying me more personalized class schedule and getting ready to apply for college. My favourite class will likekly be Advanced App Development or Stem Inquiry and Research since that's where I'll be doing most of my coding. Current Project of mine include my RPG made with pure javascript as well as my HTML5 Arcade, and a Hex turn-based game. Future Projects include a castle defense game and a platformer game.

In addition the all my other programing projects I am also involved with the Build A Metagame Project (BAM). BAM is a fakedex the was made to create a competitive metagame with a GSC-like feel. It was recently rebooted and is in the early stages, but I am involved with Pokemon creation and creating and maintaining them pokedex, I may also host a pokemon showdown server again in the future. Feel free to get involved yourself at: bamforum.tk.

As for my interests outside programming I am semi-involved in competitive Pokemon on Showdown and Pokemon Perfect. I also play a fair amount of minecraft since I enjoy the open world and all the different ways you can modify the game. Finally, I enjoy football and politics to a degree.
